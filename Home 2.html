The Dream

MUSIC IS LIFE! Mixing and Mastering is what I do. I started to seriously self-teach myself during August 2013; I was 15, turning 16, at the time. It was hard starting out because of all of the things to consider when mixing. I knew so little. I didn't know what EQ or Compression was and I didn't have an ear for being a producer. I remember coming home every day after school and just doing research on mixing and messing around with VST plugins. Over time, I got significantly better! The mixes that I do sound so better than when I first started mixing. I always go back and listen to my old work to see how far i've come along. It's so weird to me. Even now, i'm learning more of the world of being a sound engineer. I just discovered how beneficial it is to have certain equipment. My Dream, is to be in a room with legendary producers and just make timeless music for the world.
